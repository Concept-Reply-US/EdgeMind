# OVERVIEW

This guidebook provides logistical and setup information for the
ProveIt! Conference booths, and acts as a reference for additional
information. It is a living document intended to capture and communicate
conference layout, agenda, additional equipment rentals, and delivery
information. Please anticipate changes. We wanted to provide general
information now, understanding that some information will be modified
(specifically and especially assigned booths). You will receive an email
when changes are made to point you to said changes, and the last updated
date shown above will be current. If you have any questions about the
purpose of this document or comments, please reach out to our team:
[[contact@proveitconference.com]{.underline}](mailto:contact@proveitconference.com)
